Key Vocabulary

Airway
The tube air travels through on its way to the lungs.
Diaphragm
The dome-shaped muscle under the lungs that pulls air in.
Oxygen
The gas your cells need to live. It enters the blood at the alveoli.
Carbon Dioxide
Waste gas your cells make. It leaves the body when you breathe out.
