Key Vocabulary
🔄 Gas Exchange
Swap oxygen and carbon dioxide at the alveoli where air meets blood.
🔴 Inflammation
Swelling that narrows your airway from the inside.
⚡ Trigger
Thing that starts an airway attack -- dust, smoke, or exercise.
🔧 Constriction
Tightening of muscle bands that squeezes the airway shut from the outside.
