Vocabulary Reference
Airway
Tube that carries air into the lungs.
Diaphragm
Dome-shaped muscle that pulls air into the lungs.
Alveoli
Grape-cluster sacs where gas exchange happens.
Bronchi
The two branches the trachea splits into.
Trachea
The windpipe — ringed tube from throat to lungs.
Constriction
Muscle tightening that squeezes the airway shut.
Inflammation
Wall swelling that narrows the airway from inside.
Trigger
Something that starts an attack (dust, smoke, exercise).
Oxygen (O₂)
Gas the cells need to live.
Carbon Dioxide
Waste gas cells breathe out.
Gas Exchange
Oxygen and CO₂ swap at the alveoli.
